# FAQ

## Is AJT a policy engine or guardrail framework?

No. AJT does not enforce, block, or modify execution.

---

## Does AJT stop unsafe outputs?

No. AJT records judgment, not behavior.

---

## Is AJT required for EU AI Act compliance?

No. AJT is not a compliance requirement.

---

## Is this another observability tool?

No. AJT is a schema, not a backend or platform.

---

## Why not just log prompts and outputs?

Because responsibility is not reconstructable from content alone.

---

## Is AJT meant for real-time decisions?

No. AJT is post-incident oriented by design.

---

## Can I ignore AJT and still use OTel logs?

Yes. AJT is additive and optional.
